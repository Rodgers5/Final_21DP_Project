OUR ENGAGEMENTS

Support us to support the Needy Deaf.
As Deaf Individuals, we are committed to see lives of Deaf individuals and the Deaf Community change for better. Seeing most of the Deaf in life after education, lacking opportunities, we decided to step in and assist them get opportunities whenever it is available, by training them to get Life Skills, Business Skills and Computer Skills.
Our Love for the Environment.
When we ignore the environment or pollute it, we ignore our own health and neglect our future and that of our generation. It is our responsibility to keep our environment clean. CLEANLINESS IS THE SECOND TO GODLINESS. Also it is advisable to plant a tree whenever we get opportunity to, thats True Humanity both to us humans, animals and birds. Let us ssssspread the LOVE, this is what we were created for, to continue with God's work of creation. Our expected future is GREEN.
