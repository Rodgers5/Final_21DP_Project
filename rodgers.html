RODGERS SWAHILI

Founder and Chairman.

He is the Founder and Chairman on 21st Deaf Programme. He came up with the idea after completion of Core Business Skills Training, a project given tp youth by the government of kenya under KYEOP.
He had a great passion of finding a change in the lives of Deaf individuals in communities.
With lots of sacrifices he made, the Organization continues to grow and be known.
A visionary Servant-Leader with the interest of the Deaf at heart.
